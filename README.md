Pulse — AI‑Powered Customer Feedback Insights for Small Businesses
Pulse is a lightweight tool that transforms raw customer reviews into clear, actionable insights. By uploading a simple CSV file, small business owners can instantly see key themes, top complaints, top compliments, and recommendations generated by an LLM.

Why Pulse Matters for Small Businesses
Small businesses collect feedback constantly—through receipts, Google reviews, Square receipts, and in‑store conversations—but most owners don’t have the time or tools to analyze it. They often face:

Long lists of unstructured comments

No clear sense of what customers care about most

Difficulty spotting recurring issues or emerging trends

Limited access to data tools or analysts

Pulse focuses on three core needs:

Clarity: Turning dozens of reviews into a digestible summary.

Speed: Insights generated in seconds, not hours.

Accessibility: No technical skills required—just upload a CSV.

This aligns directly with Square’s mission: empowering small businesses with simple, powerful tools that help them understand their customers and make better decisions.

How Constraints Shaped the Solution
Building Pulse required working within practical constraints that influenced product decisions:

Free API usage
To avoid cost barriers, Pulse uses Groq’s free Llama‑3.1 model. This shaped the prompt design and encouraged efficient text handling while still delivering high‑quality insights.

Simple, reliable deployment
Streamlit Cloud was chosen for its free hosting, GitHub integration, and secure secret management. This allowed the project to stay focused on product behavior rather than infrastructure.

Dataset realism
A 100‑review dataset was created to mimic real restaurant feedback—balanced sentiment, varied topics, and natural language—so the demo produces meaningful insights without relying on proprietary data.

Scope discipline
To keep the project focused and shippable, the core workflow was intentionally simple:
upload → analyze → insights.
Additional features (dashboards, charts, segmentation) were intentionally left for future iterations.

How It Works
User uploads a CSV containing a review column.

The app extracts and cleans the text.

Reviews are sent to Groq’s LLM with a structured prompt.

The model returns themes, complaints, compliments, recommendations, and a weekly summary.

Results are displayed instantly in the UI.
